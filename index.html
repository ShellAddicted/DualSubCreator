How it Works?

  1. Download Subtitles for your TV Show (e.g.: from OpenSubtitles¹)
  2. Drag & Drop (or upload with 'Browse...' button) the subtitles files (.srt) in SubtitleA/B Sections
    (Place the subtitle with the best sync with your video file in SubtitleA)
  3. (Optional) Set a custom style (Color,Font,etc...)² for your subtitles.
  4. Click Generate and save the file. Done!
